# Sprint 8 — Knowledge & Learning Integration

## Mission
Integrate append-only episodes, research lifecycle, promotion gates and canonical policies.

## Definition of Done
- replay and live streams remain separated
- records are append-only and hash chained
- repeated failures create observations, not rules
- promotion gates enforce cohort and OOS requirements
- approved policy requires human approval
- canonical policy is immutable after creation
- skill update remains a proposal
- trust-aware query works
- knowledge snapshot is reproducible
- no trade-write or auto-deployment capability
