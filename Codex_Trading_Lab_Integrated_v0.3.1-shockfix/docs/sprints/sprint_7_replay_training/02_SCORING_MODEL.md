# Scoring Model v0.1

Total 100 points:

- Market Read: 20
- Scenario: 20
- Permission: 15
- Entry: 20
- Management: 15
- Process Compliance: 10

## Important
- Correct no-trade may score highly.
- Directionally correct but permission-invalid entry loses Permission and Process points.
- Manual override is scored in execution/process but gives no Entry Engine credit.
- Deterministic FAIL cannot be overridden by an AI reviewer.
