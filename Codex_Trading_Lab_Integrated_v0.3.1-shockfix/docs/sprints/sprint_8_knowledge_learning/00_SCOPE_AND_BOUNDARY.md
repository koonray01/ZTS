# Scope and Boundary

## Included
- append-only knowledge records
- episode ingestion
- research lifecycle
- failure-mode aggregation
- observations and hypotheses
- promotion gates
- locked canonical policies
- skill update proposals
- trust-aware retrieval
- knowledge snapshot exports

## Excluded
- automatic production deployment
- automatic strategy tuning
- live Codex worker
- broker execution
- statistical proof of edge
- direct mutation of skills or policies
