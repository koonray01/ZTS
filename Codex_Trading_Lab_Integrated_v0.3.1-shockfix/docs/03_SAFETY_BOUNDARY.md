# Safety Boundary

- `APPROVED` means manual-only permission for one exact snapshot/candidate and expires.
- No module may place, modify, cancel or close an order.
- Session PAUSED/LOCKED blocks new entries but may continue read-only monitoring.
- Replay, live shadow and live execution ledgers remain separate.
- Codex/model output cannot add tools or override deterministic gates.
- Canonical policy requires a new version and explicit human approval.
